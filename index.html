Biography

Soundwaves, also known as Yigit Kaya is a DJ and a Producer born in 1999. Yigit started producing when he was 14 in his bedroom. Having support from friends and family, he proceeded to release his debut single "As One" with the vocals of Stevyn on Doble Z records which was followed up by "Heart of Now" as a valentines day present. Since the release of Heart of Now, Soundwaves went missing for 2 years but as far as summer of 2020 promises, Soundwaves will bring the happiest summer vibes with his upcoming tracks. Looking forward, Soundwaves promises to deliver nothing but house music.
